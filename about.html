Hello there

5 years ago I told myself that I would like to be back in Australia. Fast forward to today and I am now blessed to be living here in the beautiful city of Sydney.

I grew up in the #westcoastbestcoast of Canada - Vancouver, B.C. I’m a former (wannabe) actor who took an alternate route and completed a Bachelor’s degree in business. The acting story is longwinded, so I'll spare you the details. The TL;DR version is that acting and producing films are hobbies now, but still very near and dear to my heart.

I’ve spent most of my professional life in the creative world of marketing and design. I stumbled into product management like many others, and I've been practicing in the space for a collective 4 years now. When it comes to building awesome things, I believe the user should always be the focal point for the decisions we make as product people.

Whenever I’m not geeking out on products, you can find me at a coffee shop learning how to code, experimenting with recipes in my instant pot, or snapping photos of people and nature. I also like to write about things on Medium and listen to throwbacks on Spotify.
